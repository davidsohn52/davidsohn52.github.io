Space Subterfuge

Created in collaboration with Blake Webb, Connor Beard, Michael Tilbury, and Scott Bays.

Watch the trailer here!

Space Subterfuge is a 3v1, asymmetric, stealth game where a band of space pirates must work together to steal data from a ship while avoiding capture by the ship guard. Strategy, teamwork, and planning are all needed if you want to succeed in one piece, or if you want to protect your precious data!
